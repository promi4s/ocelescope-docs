This page should explain what happens after a plugin method completes.

The story should be: results may be OCELs or resources, resources may render as graphs, tables, SVGs, plots, or raw structured data, and saved outputs can be reused by later plugin methods.

The page should help users understand the difference between a result artifact and its visualization.
